OUR STORY

In the Philippines, rice farming is the lifeblood of rural communities. For generations, families have depended on the land to put food on the table and send their children to school.

But the harsh reality is that life is a constant struggle. The toxic chemicals they use leave their fields barren and pose severe health risks. The high cost of these chemicals pushes farmers deeper into debt. It's a cycle of hopelessness that seems impossible to break.

At Bagor Tech, we believe that every rice farmer in the Philippines deserves a chance to break free from the cycle of poverty and debt. We understand the struggles faced by these hardworking individuals and the impact it has on their families and communities. That's why we are committed to providing them with the knowledge, tools, and support they need to transition to sustainable and natural farming methods.
